About

Bill Stack and his team have been designing unique landscapes and hardscapes throughout the Hudson Valley since 1987. Our installations are both functional and beautiful.

With stone, soil, plants, water, and light, the Mountain Gardens Landscaping Team can transform any natural area into a place you will love to be and love to show.

Our talented craftsman can also bring the timeless, organic elements of the outdoors into your home or office with creative applications for interior decoration and function.
